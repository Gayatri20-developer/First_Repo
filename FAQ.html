Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
Orders within India typically arrive in 3–5 business days. International shipments may take 7–10 days. Learn more
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. Use that link to monitor your delivery status.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?
You can return items within 14 days of delivery. Please ensure they’re unused and in original packaging. Read our full return policy.
How long until I get my refund?
Refunds are processed typically within 5–7 business days after we receive the package.
